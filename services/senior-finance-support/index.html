The problem: finance has outgrown the setup

As your business grows, the finance setup that worked before begins to slow you down. Invoices go out late. Payments become reactive. Reports take longer to prepare. Errors are harder to spot. Finance becomes a bottleneck rather than a tool.

What Senior Finance Support covers

  • Invoicing — Strengthening the way billing is reviewed, tracked, and followed through.
  • Supplier payments — Improving visibility over what needs to be paid and when.
  • Month-end reporting — A clearer reporting process and better review routine.
  • Finance controls — Reducing errors, duplication, and unclear ownership.
  • Cash visibility — Understanding what is coming in, what is going out, and what needs attention. Includes a 13-week rolling cashflow forecast.
  • Process structure — Moving finance away from messy, manual, reactive ways of working.

What you actually receive: a monthly finance pack

Each month, a clear summary of how the business is performing, where cash is heading, and what needs attention next: KPI summary with trend commentary, a 13-week cashflow forecast, variance against budget in plain English, and three clear next-step recommendations.

Frequently asked questions

How is this different from a bookkeeper or accountant?
A bookkeeper records the numbers. An accountant files them. Senior Finance Support sits at the layer above — making sure numbers are reviewed, decisions are informed by them, and the finance function is structured to scale.
What size of business is this for?
Founder-led businesses doing roughly £1m to £25m in revenue, where finance has outgrown basic bookkeeping but a full-time Finance Director is too expensive or too soon.
What's the time commitment from me as a founder?
Roughly one focused session per month, plus occasional decision calls. The point is to take finance management off the founder, not add another standing meeting.
How is pricing structured?
Scope-based. Fixed monthly fee agreed during the initial review. No hourly billing, no lock-in beyond rolling month-to-month.
